What is User Experience?

UX Design

User experience design is the art of analysing the usability of a software, taking into account features like complexity, accessibility, placement, functionality, and more. User experience design tries to simplify the product and make it as effective as possible.

UI Design

User interface design is all about the aesthetics of the website, focusing on aspects such as color, size, layout, visual design, typography, and more.
