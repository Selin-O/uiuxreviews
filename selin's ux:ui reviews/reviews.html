Instagram is a social media platform that allows interaction through direct messaging, group chats, comments, likes, and posts. Users can create an online presence with photos and captions, and keep up with their favorite celebrities as well as the news.

YouTube

Overview

YouTube is a social media platform that allows users to access videos, livestreams, premiers, access notices and news, and communication through comment sections. Users support and interact with each other by subscribing to channels they enjoy watching, liking videos, and commenting.

Features

  • Posting videos
  • Going live
  • Supporting others (by subscribing, liking, commenting)
